Further Study

  • You might have hard-coded all your compliments/insults into the HTML that was returned from your route(s). Using Python, generate a string of valid HTML that includes all the inputs from your list of compliments or insults. Appreciate how much of a pain this is, and look forward to when we learn a much much better way of doing this.

  • Embellish your form with other input types and integrate the new information that you get from your user into your response.

If you’ve gotten this far, you now have a lot of HTML as a string inside Python. Appreciate how awful this is, and look forward to when we learn a better structure for our Flask app that helps us separate these different languages and then pulls them all together for us at the end.
